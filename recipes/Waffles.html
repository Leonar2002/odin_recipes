The Third Plate

Quick Note on the History of Waffles:"Waffles or back then when they where called "Obelios are traced back to the age of the Greeks. Cooked on hot plates these spread accross Europe becoming in particular a highly mainstay dish in Belgium. "Liege Waffles" as they where called in Belgium as the Prince-Bishop took a grand liking to them.

Moving on from that quick take here is what youll need in order to begin.

What is required for this Masterpiece...

  • Eggs(2 Large Eggs)
  • Milk(1 3/4 cup milk)
  • All Purpose Flour(2 Cups)
  • White Sugar(1 Tablespoon)
  • Baking Powder(4 Teaspoons)
  • Salt(1/4 Teaspoon)
  • Vanilla Extract(1/2 Teaspoon)
  • Vegetable Oil(1/2 Cup)

How to perfectly craft this meal.

  1. Preheat waffle iron.
  2. Beat eggs in large bowl with hand beater until fluffy.
  3. Beat in flour, milk, vegetable oil, sugar, baking powder, salt and vanilla, just until smooth.
  4. Spray preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray.
  5. Pour mix onto hot waffle iron.
  6. Cook until golden brown.
