French Toast

Quick Note on the History of French Toast:"French Toast" or "Eggy Bread" was first used in 17th-century England. Soon being brought over to America by the early colonies.The people making use of stale bread giving it renewed purpose rather than being tossed away. Fast fowarding towards the 21st century where the are hundreds of different recipes and combinations of the food in the modern era. Holding especially true withing the American cuisine.

Now switching focus onto the main course these savory strips of toast will have you thirsting for more each time you eat one. This recipe may me simple but it is extravagant in its own way completely.

What is required for this Masterpiece...

  • White Bread(6 Slices)
  • Eggs(2 Large Eggs)
  • Milk(2/3 cup milk)
  • Vanilla(1 Teaspoon)
  • Cinnamon(1/4 Teaspoon)
  • Salt(A Pinch For Taste)
  • Butter(1 Tablespoon0

How to perfectly craft this meal.

  1. Combine/Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a bowl.
  2. Lightly butter a griddle or pan and heat over medium-high heat.
  3. Submerge bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.Then transfer into the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  4. Then...Enjoy your taste of the elegant.
